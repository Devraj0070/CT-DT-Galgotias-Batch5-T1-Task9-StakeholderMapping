# Stakeholder Mapping

## Course

**Critical Thinking, Design Thinking, Leadership and Teamwork**

## Topic

**Design Thinking Practices for Technical Blog Writing**

## Task 09: Stakeholder Mapping

## Selected Project

**360-Degree Digitalization of Bank Services**

---

## Project Overview

The 360-Degree Digitalization of Bank Services project aims to transform traditional banking operations into a fully digital ecosystem. The project focuses on improving customer experience, operational efficiency, security, and accessibility through modern digital technologies.

---

## Primary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder      | Role                                           | Influence        | Interest        |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ---------------- | --------------- |
| Bank Customers   | Use digital banking services                   | High Impact      | High Interest   |
| Bank Management  | Strategic decision making and project approval | High Influence   | High Interest   |
| Employees        | Daily users of banking systems                 | Medium Influence | High Interest   |
| IT Team          | Implementation, maintenance, and support       | High Influence   | High Interest   |
| Software Vendors | Provide technology solutions and services      | Medium Influence | Medium Interest |

---

## Secondary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder               | Role                                      | Influence        | Interest        |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ---------------- | --------------- |
| RBI and Regulatory Bodies | Ensure regulatory compliance              | High Influence   | Medium Interest |
| Government Agencies       | Regulatory oversight and governance       | Medium Influence | Medium Interest |
| Payment Gateway Providers | Support secure digital transactions       | Medium Influence | Medium Interest |
| Cybersecurity Teams       | Protect customer and organizational data  | High Influence   | High Interest   |
| Investors                 | Monitor business growth and profitability | Medium Influence | Medium Interest |

---

## Power-Interest Matrix

### Manage Closely (High Power, High Interest)

* Bank Management
* IT Team
* Cybersecurity Teams

### Keep Satisfied (High Power, Medium Interest)

* RBI and Regulatory Bodies
* Software Vendors
* Investors

### Keep Informed (Medium Power, High Interest)

* Bank Customers
* Employees

### Monitor (Medium Power, Medium Interest)

* Government Agencies
* Payment Gateway Providers

---

## Conclusion

The successful implementation of 360-Degree Digitalization of Bank Services requires active collaboration among all stakeholders. Bank management, IT teams, cybersecurity professionals, and customers play critical roles in ensuring project success. Regulatory compliance, secure digital infrastructure, and continuous stakeholder engagement are essential for delivering a reliable and efficient digital banking experience.
